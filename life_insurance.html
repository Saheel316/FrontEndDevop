Life Insurance Checklist

Listed below are the important factors on which Life Insurance Premium depends.

  • Accidental Death Benefit
  • Death Benefit
  • Critical Illness Benefit
  • Portability
  • Waiver Of Premiums in case of Permanent Disability
  • Acceleration Of death Benefit on Terminal Illness
  • Top Up Option
  • Claim Settlement Ratio
  • Return of Premium on Maturity

Premium varies from company to company. Thus, it is best to talk to an expert to find the best insurance for you.
