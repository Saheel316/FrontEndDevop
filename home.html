About Us

Affluent is a community which is structured on 3 core pillars, which are its Members, Members Care, Members Gains.All the products are designed keeping these 3 parameters in mind.

Our goal is to provide a layer of care to our members and also establish an alternative line of income for our members.This COVID has confirmed our belief that every individual will look for 2 Key things.

Protection

To meet the unforseen expense

Alternative source of income

To add cash flows to current income

Our Objectives

✓Alternative source of cash flow

✓Low Investement

✓Good Returns & Reduced Risks

✓Capturing the theory of Demand & Supply

At present we are working in segments like Insurance which is about the care of affluent members.We help our members achieve layer protection for themselves and their loved ones. In the process we also provide Finance to our members to buy this protection and that too at 0%.
